# 4. Flight Execution

When in position for takeoff, ground control open, and all above steps followed:

> [!NOTE]
> **Manual takeoff and landing are recommended.**

* **Start UAV from controller:**
    * **ARM the UAV:** Hold down on the left joystick while pressing the **ARM** button for a few seconds. The propellers will start to move!
    * To take off, move the **left joystick up**.
* Manually takeoff to **75% of flight altitude**.
* Point UAV away from polygon (Green lights front, red lights back).
* **Initiate GPS:**
    * Fly at max speed 3 sec in one direction and 3 sec back, turn UAV around and repeat.
* Go to approx. flight altitude and start mission:
    * Press **A** for autoflight.
* UAV will return to launch when done.
* Manually land.
* **DISARM UAV:** left joystick down for 10 sec & press **DISARM** button.
* Confirm UAV is disarmed.
